Why homeowners choose LuxCraft

Your resale flat, renewed with end-to-end care

We treat every resale renovation as both upgrade and restoration—resolving inherited issues, optimising space for your routines, and layering materials that feel timeless the day you move back in.

  1. 01

    Reconfiguration with purpose — We study existing walls, beams, and trunking to unlock better circulation, daylight, and zoning while complying with HDB structural guidelines.

  2. 02

    Wet-area renewal — Bathrooms and kitchens get full waterproofing checks, new plumbing runs, and ventilation upgrades to extend the life of every finish.

  3. 03

    Custom storage for every odd corner — Bespoke carpentry, display shelving, and built-ins maximise niches and recesses common in older flats.

  4. 04

    Certified specialists & compliance — Licensed electricians, plumbers, and gas fitters handle all technical scopes with proper permits and photo updates.

  5. 05

    Transparency from costing to handover — Expect clear allowances, weekly site reports, and a dedicated point of contact before, during, and after renovation.
