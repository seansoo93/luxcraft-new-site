What renovation services do you offer?

LuxCraft provides a comprehensive range of renovation services including hacking and demolition, carpentry, painting, glass and metal work, masonry and tiling, electrical work, and plumbing. We handle every aspect of your renovation project with expertise and precision.

Do you provide 3D renderings before work begins?

Yes, we offer complimentary 3D renderings of your design concept before starting any project. This ensures your vision is accurately captured and brought to life.

How do I schedule a meeting?

Schedule a meeting by contacting us via WhatsApp or phone at +65 8312 6212 (Shamim) or +65 8899 1245 (Manfred). Meetings are strictly by appointment due to our frequent on-site engagements.

Are your subcontractors certified?

Yes, all our subcontractors are certified and registered in their respective domains, such as hacking, tiling, carpentry, plumbing, and electrical works. We prioritise compliance with industry standards and regulations.
