5-Room BTO @ Tengah Drive

Biophilic textures, sculpted timber details, and relaxed lighting turn this new Tengah flat into a grounded sanctuary.

Biophilic Warmth & Flexible Zones

The Tengah Drive 5-room BTO embraces natural materials and fluid space planning. A timber-clad feature wall conceals storage while framing indoor greenery, and a sliding glass enclosure lets the study expand into the living room for larger gatherings. The kitchen balances terrazzo-look counters with matte sage cabinetry and open shelving, keeping the palette light yet tactile. Soft cove lighting and angled carpenter-made seating anchor the dining hub, encouraging slow breakfasts and late-night conversations alike.
