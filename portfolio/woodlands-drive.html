4-Room Resale @ Woodlands Drive

Rounded edges, quiet neutrals, and generous storage reinvent this Woodlands resale flat into a calm family retreat.

Soft Curves & Concealed Storage

This Woodlands Drive resale home layers gentle curves across the living core, from a sweeping media wall to fluted cabinetry that hides ample storage. A built-in bench anchors the dining nook while keeping circulation free, and ribbed glass doors borrow light for the study without compromising privacy. In the kitchen, matte pebble fronts, quartz counters, and warm task lighting create an inviting workspace that transitions seamlessly into the communal zone.
