3BR Condo @ Sengkang

A riverside condo refreshed with coastal hues, curved joinery, and breezy lighting for a laid-back family home.

Coastal Calm & Tailored Storage

Overlooking Sengkang Riverside Park, this three-bedroom condo leans into relaxed resort cues. The living room features a sweeping console in light oak that hides media clutter and curves toward a bay-window reading perch. In the kitchen, powder-blue cabinetry, reeded glass uppers, and brushed brass pulls brighten the galley layout, while a quartz peninsula doubles as a breakfast spot. The primary suite pairs panelled wardrobes with woven textures and dimmable sconces to keep evenings calm, and the study nook conceals a Murphy desk so the space pivots effortlessly between work and guests.
