    FAQ

    What services does LuxCraft offer?

    LuxCraft provides a comprehensive range of renovation services including hacking and demolition, carpentry, painting, glass and metal work, masonry and tiling, electrical work, and plumbing. We handle every aspect of your renovation project with expertise and precision.

    Do you offer design consultations?

    Yes, we offer complimentary 3D renderings of your design concept before starting any project. This ensures that your vision is accurately captured and brought to life, providing you with a clear visual representation of the final outcome.
