About LuxCraft

Your go-to interior designer in Singapore

Welcome to LuxCraft, your go-to interior designer in Singapore. We specialise in hacking, carpentry, painting, glass and metal work, masonry, tiling, electrical work, and plumbing-every trade you need to bring a timeless space to life.

We offer complimentary 3D renderings of your design concept before starting any project, ensuring your vision is perfectly realised. At LuxCraft, renovation dreams are crafted with precision, excellence, and sincere collaboration.

Our philosophy

Every project starts by listening deeply to how you live. We translate those insights into future-ready layouts, mood-led palettes, and bespoke carpentry that feels personal from day one.

Your design lead stays with you through selections, approvals, and on-site coordination, ensuring a clear point of contact at every milestone.

How we work

An integrated renovation journey

From the first consultation, we map out a clear process so you know exactly what to expect-from compliance checks to materials, schedules, and styling.

  • Complimentary site recce, proposal, and 3D renderings so decisions are confident and informed.
  • Dedicated project lead coordinating certified subcontractors across carpentry, electrical, plumbing, and wet works.
  • Progress walkthroughs and snag rectification to make sure handover day feels effortless.

Our capabilities

Integrated services include hacking and demolition, bespoke carpentry, premium painting systems, glass and metal fabrications, artisan masonry and tiling, certified electrical work, and licensed plumbing.

We're equally at home with HDB BTO homes, spacious landed properties, and commercial interiors that call for a distinctive brand experience.

Design leadership

Our senior designers guide mood direction, material selection, and space planning with a balance of aesthetics and practicality.

Project governance

Every timeline, permit, and site update is tracked so workflows stay compliant and transparent.
